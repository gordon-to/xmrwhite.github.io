Hi, I like to bring ideas to production.

I'm a student at University of Toronto working towards a BSc in Computer Science. I began programming at my high school where I was introduced to the One Laptop per Child initiative. This program brought me all the way to Isiolo, in Kenya, where I deployed a classroom set of laptops in 2009. I returned three times to grow the collection to over 80 computers that are still being used today. I'm always looking for new ways to solve big problems.

My Projects

I've been a technical consultant to the locations and finance departments on multiple productions. I enjoy the Film and Television production where everyone works together to make a big idea come to life.

I work to support an OLPC project in Kenya as well as OLPC Canada. We bring laptops to children who would otherwise be unable to use technology or the Internet to improve their learning.

I'm currently the treasurer of the Computer Science Student Union at the University of Toronto

Agnico Eagle is one of Canada's most successful gold mining companies. I've been working on a custom Industrial Hygiene dashboard built on SharePoint.
